Welcome to Party Yorkies of the Grove! We are a family owned and run business dedicated to breeding Parti Yorkshire Terriers. We stand out from other Yorkshire Terriers because of our dogs' unique coloring. We breed puppies that are black, white and tan. They are a tiny 5 to 7 pounds once fully grown making them perfect dogs to take with you out on the town.

These dogs might be small, but they are mighty. They are classified under the Toy Group due to their deminished size. They don't always realize just how small they are and have a larger than life attitude. It's advised that you keep an eye on them around big dogs. Because of their statute it's important that you don't let them develope "Small Dog-Syndrome". This is seen when owners allow tiny pups to get away with things when they misbehave.

These dogs are intelligent, stubborn and energetic creatures. It's important to give them attention when training as well as stimulating their energetic tendencies with walks or playtime. By nature these are long haired dogs. Most owners will keep their hair short for convienence,
